# Literature Map

## Core thesis space

- Tactile sensing has been used for contact detection, slip detection, state estimation, force control, and visuo-tactile manipulation.
- Recent work moves toward tactile/world models and contact-rich policies, but mostly treats tactile as an observation stream rather than a causal diagnostic signal.
- The likely wedge is separating internal model error from exogenous contact disturbances through tactile residuals.
